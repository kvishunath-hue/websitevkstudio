Films & Content
How we'd produce the three Modern India films + motion graphics
Our workflow

1. Script (you provide). Curatorial team writes 180-sec script for each film. Includes shot-by-shot beats, Ambedkar quotes, archival references.

2. Storyboard. We turn the script into a scene-by-scene storyboard. Quick approval cycle.

3. AI generation. Each scene rough-cut with AI (Veo 3.1 Fast for Standard, Sora 2 Pro for Premium). Multiple variants per scene.

4. Real footage blend. Where archival or contemporary footage exists, we cut it in. Modern banks, factories, infrastructure shots — sourced from stock libraries or our own photography.

5. Motion graphics. Data viz, kinetic typography for quotes, infographic moments.

6. Voice-over (optional). Your call at script stage — TTS, AI-cloned voice, or human voice-over artist.

7. Score & sound design. Light orchestral or Indian classical underscore. 5.1 surround mix.

8. Final render. 4K MP4 + 5.1 audio mix, ready for projector loop.

AI token cost · pass-through transparency

Standard tier (Veo 3.1 Fast) · $0.10/sec · ~$13 per film · ~₹1,100 per film · ~₹3,300 total for 3 films

Premium tier (Sora 2 Pro) · $0.30–0.40/sec · ~$50 per film · ~₹4,200 per film · ~₹12,600 total for 3 films

Token cost is negligible at this scale. The cost driver is our labour: storyboarding, curation across multiple variants, revisions, motion graphics, voice direction, sound design.

No teaser policy

Most film production processes include a "teaser" before commitment. We don't. Once your script is approved, we go directly to full production.

This saves ~2 weeks per film. The decision risk shifts to script approval, which is the right place for it — script is what defines the film, not the teaser.

You get 3 revision rounds (Standard) or 4 rounds (Premium) on the full film, which is where you have real control.

Scope-creep rate card

If you want changes beyond what's included:

· Additional film minute beyond agreed length: ₹1.0 L (Std) · ₹2.0 L (Prem)

· Additional revision round beyond included: ₹0.3 L (Std) · ₹0.5 L (Prem)

· Additional language for voice-over: ₹1.0 L (Std) · ₹1.5 L (Prem)

All change orders documented and approved before work begins.
